Virtual Reality (VR)

Virtual reality is a computer-generated, simulated environment that immerses a user by providing realistic sights, sounds, and other sensations, often through a headset that tracks movements and adjusts the view in real-time. It creates a sense of presence, making users feel as though they are inside a different world, which can be used for gaming, training, education, and more.
